# Kinetic Abstract ## Product Overview **The Pitch:** Lowkal.fm is an immersive, art-forward community radio experience. It discards traditional web navigation in favor of a fluid, living digital canvas that prioritizes sonic discovery and visual mood over conventional structure. **For:** Underground music enthusiasts and digital art appreciators who want a distraction-free, aesthetically engaging listening experience. **Device:** desktop **Design Direction:** A high-energy, fluid kinetic abstract direction featuring morphing organic shapes, stark high contrast, and freely drifting navigational elements. **Inspired by:** NTS Radio, Poolside FM, abstract WebGL experiments. --- ## Screens - **Homepage (Live Player):** Full-bleed fluid motion canvas with a central floating player widget and minimal archive portal. - **Archive (Drifting Cloud):** Asymmetrical, freely-floating physics-based canvas of mix covers drifting in space. - **Mix Detail (Expanded Player):** Focused listening view with tracklist and host details overlaying the abstract background. - **About/Info Overlay:** Minimalist glassmorphic panel detailing station manifest and schedule. --- ## Key Flows **Enter the Archive:** User transitions from live broadcast to past mixes. 1. User is on Homepage -> sees fluid background, central live player, and small pulsing circle bottom-right. 2. User clicks bottom-right pulsing circle (`Archive` portal) -> Background shifts color, live player shrinks/fades. 3. Drifting cloud of mix covers fades in and begins floating across the viewport. **Play an Archived Mix:** User selects a mix from the cloud. 1. User is on Archive (Drifting Cloud) -> sees slowly floating square artworks. 2. User clicks an artwork -> Other artworks scatter outward. 3. Selected mix scales up, expands into Mix Detail player, and audio begins. ---
Design System ## Color Palette - **Primary:** `#D5FF00` - Electric Lime (Play buttons, active states, cursors) - **Background:** `#050511` - Ink Blue (Deep canvas base) - **Surface:** `#14142B` - Deep Violet (Translucent widgets, floating panels) - **Text:** `#FFFFFF` - Pure White (Main typography) - **Muted:** `#8A8A9D` - Ash Blue (Timestamps, secondary info) - **Accent:** `#FF0055` - Neon Magenta (Live indicator, morphing blob gradient stops) ## Typography - **Headings:** `Syne`, 800, 32-64px - **Body:** `Outfit`, 400, 16px - **Small text:** `Outfit`, 300, 12px - **Buttons:** `Syne`, 700, 14px, uppercase **Style notes:** Aggressive border radii (`32px` on floating panels), severe backdrop blur (`24px`), stark contrast (electric lime on ink blue). No hard borders; everything is defined by glow and shadow (`0 12px 48px rgba(0,0,0,0.6)`). Custom cursor replacing default pointer with an inverted lime dot. ## Design Tokens ```css :root { --color-primary: #D5FF00; --color-background: #050511; --color-surface: rgba(20, 20, 43, 0.4); --color-text: #FFFFFF; --color-muted: #8A8A9D; --color-accent: #FF0055; --font-display: 'Syne', sans-serif; --font-ui: 'Outfit', sans-serif; --radius-panel: 32px; --radius-pill: 99px; --spacing-sm: 8px; --spacing-md: 16px; --spacing-lg: 32px; --blur-heavy: blur(24px); --shadow-float: 0 16px 48px rgba(0,0,0,0.8); } ```

Screen Specifications ### Homepage (Live Player) **Purpose:** Primary entry point playing the live community radio broadcast. **Layout:** Full viewport canvas. No header, no footer. Central floating player. Bottom-right archive trigger. Top-left discreet logo. **Key Elements:** - **Background Canvas:** WebGL or CSS-animated morphing blobs. `#050511` base with slow-moving `#FF0055` and `#D5FF00` soft-edged gradients. - **Live Player Widget:** Centered floating pill. `400px` wide, `120px` tall. `--color-surface` with `--blur-heavy`. - **Play/Pause Button:** `64x64px` circle, `#D5FF00` background, black icon. - **Now Playing Text:** `Syne` 800, `24px`. Marquee scrolling if too long. - **Archive Portal:** Bottom right. `80px` circle, pulsing outline. Hover reveals "ENTER ARCHIVE". **States:** - **Loading:** Background is static ink blue. Simple electric lime spinner in center. - **Error:** Player widget reads "STREAM OFFLINE" in `--color-accent`. **Components:** - **Live Indicator:** `8x8px` pulsing `#FF0055` dot next to "LIVE" text. **Interactions:** - **Hover Play:** Button scales to `1.1`. - **Hover Archive:** Circle expands to `120px`, text fades in, background motion speeds up slightly. **Responsive:** - **Desktop:** Centered layout, massive background elements. --- ### Archive (Drifting Cloud) **Purpose:** Unstructured, discovery-focused browsing of past radio mixes. **Layout:** Full viewport. No grid. Items float across the screen at varying depths (sizes and speeds). **Key Elements:** - **Canvas:** Darker `#050511` background, less color bleeding than homepage to make artworks pop. - **Mix Nodes:** Square album artworks. Sizes vary (`150px`, `250px`, `350px`) to simulate depth. - **Back/Exit Node:** Top left floating pill. `120x40px`, reads "BACK TO LIVE". **States:** - **Empty:** "ARCHIVE EMPTY" text floating in center. - **Loading:** Nodes fade in one by one from random opacities. **Components:** - **Mix Node:** Image container, `12px` border radius, `--shadow-float`. **Interactions:** - **Hover Node:** Node pauses movement. Play icon (`#D5FF00`) fades in over center. Z-index increases. - **Click Node:** Triggers transition to Mix Detail screen. **Responsive:** - **Desktop:** 15-20 nodes visible at once, complex physics. --- ### Mix Detail (Expanded Player) **Purpose:** Focused playback of an archived mix with contextual information. **Layout:** Split focus. Artwork large on left, tracklist/info scrolling panel on right. **Key Elements:** - **Artwork Panel:** `400x400px` cover art, perfectly centered vertically on left half. - **Info Panel:** Right half. Glassmorphic `--color-surface` container, `480px` wide, `80vh` tall. - **Title:** `Syne` 800, `48px`, `#FFFFFF`. - **Scrubber:** Linear progress bar. `2px` thick track (`#8A8A9D`), `4px` thick progress (`#D5FF00`), `16px` handle. - **Tracklist:** Vertical list of timestamps and track names. `Outfit`, `14px`. **States:** - **Loading:** Skeleton pulses in tracklist area. **Components:** - **Track Item:** Hover background `rgba(213, 255, 0, 0.1)`, `8px` padding, left-aligned timestamp in `--color-primary`. **Interactions:** - **Click Timestamp:** Scrubber jumps to time, audio skips. **Responsive:** - **Desktop:** Left/right split perfectly balanced. --- ### About/Info Overlay **Purpose:** Station manifesto and schedule details without leaving the current context. **Layout:** Centered modal overlay covering 70% of the screen. **Key Elements:** - **Backdrop:** `#050511` at `80%` opacity, heavily blurred. - **Content Container:** `800px` max-width. `--color-surface`, `1px` border of `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`. - **Header:** "LOWKAL MANIFESTO" in `Syne` 800, `64px`. - **Schedule Grid:** 2-column layout mapping out weekly resident DJs. **States:** - **Static:** Only state required. **Components:** - **Close Button:** `48x48px` circular button, top right corner of overlay. `#FFFFFF` "X" icon. **Interactions:** - **Click Close:** Overlay slides down `40px` and fades out to `0` opacity over `300ms`. **Responsive:** - **Desktop:** Centered floating card.

Build Guide **Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3 + GSAP (for fluid layout/drifting archive) + Three.js (optional, for background morphing) **Build Order:** 1. **Homepage (Live Player):** Establishes the core WebGL/CSS morphing background, primary font stacking, and the aesthetic anchor. 2. **About/Info Overlay:** Easy win to set up glassmorphism tokens (`--blur-heavy`, `--color-surface`). 3. **Archive (Drifting Cloud):** The most complex engineering task. Requires setting up a randomized physics/drift loop for the DOM elements. 4. **Mix Detail:** Standard UI implementation reusing components from the homepage player and overlay.
